## Locked Direction
- **Palette**: Warm workshop palette (parchment + terracotta/copper)
- **Typography (site)**: Instrument Serif + DM Sans
- **Logo**: Original `工` mark + **Koubou Labs** wordmark in **Cormorant Garamond (Option A)**

- **Logo composition**: **Stacked top-bottom**
